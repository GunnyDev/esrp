How to apply

The more ideas you have for a character and their story/backstory the better - if you only write a few lines, your application will be rejected. You must write at least 500 words, and have some good ideas about what has happened to the character in the past, what they might be doing now, etc.

Your form will be reviewed and you will get a decision within approx 1-5 days. Geordie will post in the applications channel to say who has been accepted and rejected. Please be patient. If your application is rejected, you may re-apply.

Please note, you must be age 18+ to join. The server team reserve the right to deny access. You must also read the server rules which are included on this application link.

To play you will need to install FiveM, if you need help with this, there is a video here

The form asks for your Steam 64 Hex ID. to find this please go to Steam Finder and input your Steam ID. You should be given a long number starting with 1, that may have letters in it.

Server restarts happen every day at around 9am and 6pm, for updates and fixes. They can also happen at other times as announced in gta-chat channel. You will not be able to use the server for 5-10 mins at these times.

Good Luck!
